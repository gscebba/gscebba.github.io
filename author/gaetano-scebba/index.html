Gaetano Scebba

Doctoral Researcher

ETH Zurich

I am a Doctoral Researcher at ETH Zurich, Switzerland. My research focuses on advancing physiological monitoring by utilizing artificial intelligence and data from cameras and wearable devices. Thanks to the great interconnected environment of ETH, I work in close collaboration with medical experts and researchers from Balgrist Clinic and University Hospital Zurich.

My long-term research interests center around the digital transformation of health care by utilizing machine learning and heterogeneous health data to better manage and treat chronic diseases.

Research Interests

Biomedical Informatics, Digital Health, AI for Healthcare
