About me

I am a PhD student in Biomedical Engineering at the Mobile Health Systems Laboratory of ETH Zurich, Switzerland. My research focuses on advancing physiological monitoring by utilizing artificial intelligence and data from cameras and wearable devices. Thanks to the great interconnected environment of ETH, I work in close collaboration with medical experts and researchers from Balgrist Clinic and University Hospital Zurich.

My long term mission is to build intelligent technologies for digital health by using computer vision and machine learning methods.
